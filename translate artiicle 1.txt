Moataz Al-Battawi, General Supervisor of Soccer at Ceramica Cleopatra Club, said that Saleh Jumaa, a former Al-Ahly player and recently joined the ranks of the newly promoted Premier League team, promised to restore his usual form.
Ceramica Cleopatra rose for the first time to the Egyptian Premier League, and the club's management signed 20 deals to support the team, including Saleh Jumaa.
قال معتز البطاوي المشرف العام على الكرة بنادي سيراميكا كليوباترا، إن صالح جمعة لاعب الأهلي السابق والمنضم مؤخرًا إلى صفوف الفريق الصاعد حديثًا للدوري الممتاز وعد باستعادة مستواه المعهود.
وصعد سيراميكا كليوباترا لأول مرة إلى الدوري المصري الممتاز، وقامت إدارة النادي بالتعاقد مع 20 صفقة لتدعيم صفوف الفريق من بينهم صالح جمعة

During radio statements for the "Al-Saqr Stadium" program, Al-Battawi said: "We are confident in the abilities of Saleh Jumaa, as a player, as he is a great addition to the team, and the player promised to restore his level under the leadership of Haitham Shaaban (the team coach), and we are waiting for his return to the Egyptian national team."
He continued: "We have concluded good deals. We hope to provide a good level during the league matches, and tomorrow's match against El Entag El Harby is historic for us."
He concluded: "We hope to win the match. Our ambitions are great and have no limits, but we are taking distinguished steps and we do not want to anticipate events."
It is worth noting that the Egyptian We League championship will start tomorrow, Friday, with a match between Enppi and Aswan at Petrosport Stadium.
